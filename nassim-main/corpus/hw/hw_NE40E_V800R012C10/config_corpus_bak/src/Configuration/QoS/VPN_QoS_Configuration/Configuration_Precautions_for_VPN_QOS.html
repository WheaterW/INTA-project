VPN QoS does not support ECMP or TI-LFA for SR-MPLS TE. If VPN QoS recurses to SR-MPLS TE tunnels and ECMP or TI-LFA protection is configured for the tunnels, the protection does not take effect. Proper deployment is recommended.

When the public network tunnel for VPN QoS is a TE tunnel, a TE tunnel change (for example, the TE HSB LSP goes down or up) may cause a small number of VPN packets to be lost.
